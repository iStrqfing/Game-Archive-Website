Game of the Week

A new game just dropped. Call of Duty®: Modern Warfare® II is the sequel to 2019’s blockbuster Modern Warfare®. Featuring the return of the iconic, team leader Captain John Price, the fearless John "Soap" MacTavish, the seasoned Sergeant Kyle "Gaz” Garrick, and the lone wolf himself, fan favorite Simon “Ghost” Riley, players will witness what makes Task Force 141 the legendary squad it is today. From small-scale, high-stakes infiltration tactics to highly classified missions, players will need to leverage new weapons, vehicles, and cutting-edge equipment to take on their enemies. Gear up and take the fight offshore as you siege enemies underwater, breach a highly fortified enemy base, infil along the canals, and liberate much-needed allies at a black site hidden within the mountains.

Tip of the Week

“One of the most important factors in success in FPS games is your crosshair placement. Make sure your crosshair is always at head height and pointed towards areas where enemies are likely to appear. This will give you a split-second advantage in gunfights and allow you to take down opponents more quickly and efficiently. Additionally, make sure to practice adjusting your crosshair placement as you move around the map and encounter different situations. With practice and consistency, you'll see a significant improvement in your gameplay.”
